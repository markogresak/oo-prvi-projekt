2. rekonceptualizacija

Video instalacija kot instrumentalizacija medija videa in njegova povezava s klasičnimi umetniškimi mediji v novo kontekstualno povezavo oziroma generirano podobo.
Od leve proti desni:

  1. reprodukcija ohridske ikone Marijinega oznanjenja, okoli 1300,
  2. NEO GEO, video, barvni, zvočni, 5', 1989,
  3. NEO GEO VII in NEO GEO VIII, video instalaciji, ekrani, les, kovina, barva, 1989.

Video installation as the instrumentalization of video as a medium and its connecting with classic art media into a new contextual whole – a generated image.
From left to right:

  1. reproduction of the Ohrid icon of the Annunciation, around 1300,
  2. video NEO GEO, color, sound, 5', 1989,
  3. video installation NEO GEO 7 and NE0 GEO 8, screens, wood, metal, paint, 1989.
