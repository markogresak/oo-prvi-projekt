2. rekonceptualizacija

Voajer, ekshibicionist, narcis, od osebnega k javnemu, prvi vstop v umetnost 80-ih, video dela na javni TV.
Od leve proti desni:

  • Nuša & Srečo Dragan, Image no Door, 4', 35-milimetrska delavnica, RTV Slovenija, 1982,
  • Nuša & Srečo Dragan, Video šus, 3', Pokretne slike, RTV Beograd, 1982,
  • Nuša & Srečo Dragan, Spomenik žrtev in orgije, 7', oddaja Dobre vibracije, TV Sarajevo, 1985.

Voyeur, exhibitionist, narcissist, from personal to public, first steps in the art of the 1980s, video works on public TV.
From left to right:

  • Nuša & Srečo Dragan, Image no Door, 4', 35-milimetrska delavnica (35-Millimeter Workshop) TV program, RTV Slovenija, 1982,
  • Nuša & Srečo Dragan, Video Shoes, 3', Pokretne slike (Moving Pictures) TV program, RTV Beograd, 1982,
  • Nuša & Srečo Dragan, Monument of Sacrifice and Orgy, 7', Dobre vibracije (Good Vibrations) TV program, TV Sarajevo, 1985.
