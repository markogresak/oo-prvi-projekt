Soba 1 / Room 1

Konceptna tabla VII / Concept Board 7

3. rekonceptualizacija

Gibljive slike, ki se kontekstualizirajo kot perceptivne in programirane situacije performansa v mediju filma (the expanded cinema) in OHO-jevih Poletnih projektih (land art).
Od leve proti desni:

  1. koncept, fotografije in filmski skript – risbe pozicij premikanja za film,
  2. prikazuje se film 72 ur zime, 72 ur jeseni, park Tivoli, Ljubljana, 1969,
  3. koncept in fotodokumentacija projekta 16 m2 ali 40 x 50 cm peska pod steklom na Zemlji 21. junija 1969 ob 15. uri pred Moderno galerijo v Ljubljani.

Moving pictures contextualized as perceptive and programmed situations of performance in film (the expanded cinema) and Summer Projects by OHO (land art).
From left to right:

  1. concept, photographs and film script – sketches for a storyboard,
  2. screening of the film 72 Hours of Winter, 72 Hours of Autumn, Tivoli Park, Ljubljana, 1969,
  3. concept and photo-documentation of the project 16 m2 or 80 x 40 x 50 cm of Gravel Under Glass on Earth, 21st June 1969, at 3 p.m., in front of the Museum of Modem Art in Ljubljana.
